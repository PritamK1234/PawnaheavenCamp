Revenue Prompt

You are working on the Admin Revenue page of our villa booking platform.

Goal: Replace the hardcoded values with real calculated data from the database.

Financial logic of our platform:

1. Customer Payment
   Customers only pay 30% advance at booking time.

Example
Villa price = ₹10,000
Customer pays = ₹3,000 (advance)

Only this 30% advance counts as revenue in the admin system.

---

2. Gross Revenue

Gross Revenue =
Sum of all advance payments received from confirmed bookings for the selected month.

Exclude:

 Cancelled bookings before payment
 Failed payments

Include:

 Completed bookings
 Upcoming bookings (if advance already paid)

---

3. Refund Payable

Refund Payable =
Total refunds we must return to customers for that month.

Cases:

 Booking cancelled after payment
 Admin approved refund

---

4. Referral Payable

Referral Payable =
Total commission earned by all referral partners for bookings in that month.

Rules:

 Commission calculated per booking
 Only count confirmed bookings
 If referral already withdrawn, exclude it
 Only show pending payable referral amount

---

5. Net Revenue

Net Revenue = Gross Revenue - Refund Payable - Referral Payable

---

6. Admin Revenue Split

After calculating Net Revenue:

Admin A = 70% of Net Revenue
Admin B = 30% of Net Revenue

Example:

Net Revenue = ₹10,00,000

Admin A = ₹7,00,000
Admin B = ₹3,00,000

---

refund_date
